The Drowning Swimmer

The weight of its wetness
stretches the skin of her face
Marvelling at her ability
she progresses on her brand-new skill
Head-on into the deep
she surges, relishing lightness and wetness
Robust sun signals of
yet another shining bright new day
Chirping birds fly past
as the lanky guard saunters aimlessly
Midway in the deep
every cell and follicle await signal, while
Mind seized with anguish
Furiously presses alarm button inviting danger
Instructions evaporate; breathing abates
water-water everywhere leaving lungs no air
Gasping a brave bid
popping head up; feeble palm signal
The life guard now registers
There drowns a swimmer
Despair on both swimmer and saver
Theory vanishes; reality strikes
Leaving bamboo pole and life’s dole
Jumps into the pool
in a brave bid to save!
The two struggling novices
one heaving while the other clutching
Move but an inch
to the horror of early swimmers
Written in the fate
Will to live; time to leave
Winning hands clutches side-bar
huffing and puffing for fresh air
Both experiences life lesson As wetness of water floats a new-life.
